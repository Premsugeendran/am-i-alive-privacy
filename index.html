Privacy Policy – Am I Alive

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Am I Alive is a personal safety and wellbeing application designed to help users check in regularly, track moods, manage reminders, and notify trusted contacts if prolonged inactivity is detected. Your privacy and data security are extremely important to us.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your data.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Email address (used for secure sign-in via email magic link)

2. Profile Information

  • Name (for personalization within the app)
  • Time zone
  • Emergency contact details (name, phone number, optional email)

3. App Usage & Safety Data

  • Check-in timestamps (e.g., “I am alive” activity)
  • Mood entries you choose to record
  • Pill or reminder schedules you configure
  • Inactivity status used to trigger reminders or alerts

4. Security Information

  • Encrypted PIN credentials (stored as a secure hash)
  • Failed unlock attempt counters (used for security lockout)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Protect access to the app using a local PIN
  • Enable safety check-ins and inactivity detection
  • Send reminders and alerts you explicitly enable
  • Notify your selected emergency contacts if inactivity thresholds are reached
  • Improve app reliability and user experience

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or tracking.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

Your data is shared only in the following limited cases:

  • With your chosen emergency contacts, if inactivity alerts are triggered based on your settings
  • With trusted infrastructure providers (such as email or messaging services) strictly to operate app functionality

Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted communication (HTTPS)
  • Secure server-side hashing for PINs
  • Rate limiting and lockout protection
  • Restricted access to internal tools

Your Choices & Rights

You can:

  • Edit or delete your profile information
  • Update or remove emergency contacts
  • Enable or disable reminders and alerts
  • Request account deletion

To request support or account deletion, contact us at:
amialive.support@gmail.com

Important Disclaimers

  • Am I Alive is not a replacement for emergency services.
  • Medication reminders are informational only and do not constitute medical advice.
  • Always follow your doctor’s instructions and prescriptions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
amialive.support@gmail.com
